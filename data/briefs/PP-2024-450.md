## What's being proposed

This proposal, put forward by Legacy [doc: PP-2024-450 - Draft Public Benefit Offer | p.4], aims to change the planning rules for a site in Dural [doc: PP-2024-450 - Planning Proposal | p.32]. The proposal also includes a 32-meter wide road reservation through the site, intended to accommodate a future bypass road connecting to Old Northern Road [doc: PP-2024-450 - Traffic and Transport Impact Assessment | p.6][doc: PP-2024-450 - Planning Proposal | p.84][doc: PP-2024-450 - Draft Indicative Site Specific DCP | p.9][doc: PP-2024-450 - Urban Design Report | p.29]. Beyond the homes, the proposal includes creating a new central local park [doc: PP-2024-450 - Planning Proposal | p.32][doc: PP-2024-450 - Urban Design Report | p.29], a pedestrian link [doc: PP-2024-450 - Draft Public Benefit Offer | p.2][doc: PP-2024-450 - Urban Design Report | p.29], and improving access for Dural Public School [doc: PP-2024-450 - Planning Proposal | p.32][doc: PP-2024-450 - Urban Design Report | p.29]. It also involves building a new local collector road with a 32-meter wide reserve [doc: PP-2024-450 - Planning Proposal | p.32][doc: PP-2024-450 - Traffic and Transport Impact Assessment | p.25][doc: PP-2024-450 - Planning Proposal | p.84][doc: PP-2024-450 - Draft Indicative Site Specific DCP | p.9][doc: PP-2024-450 - Urban Design Report | p.40], and a new intersection on Old Northern Road [doc: PP-2024-450 - Draft Public Benefit Offer | p.4][doc: PP-2024-450 - Planning Proposal | p.84][doc: PP-2024-450 - Draft Indicative Site Specific DCP | p.9][doc: PP-2024-450 - Urban Design Report | p.37].

## What changes on the ground

This proposal involves specific changes to the planning rules for the sites at 614, 618, 626 Old Northern Road and 21, 27 Derriwong Road, Dural [doc: PP-2024-450 - Planning Proposal | p.42][doc: PP-2024-450 - Exhibition Fact Sheet | p.2]. Here's what would change on the ground: * **Zoning**: The current RU6 Transition zone [doc: PP-2024-450 - Planning Proposal | p.87] would be changed to R2 Low Density Residential [doc: PP-2024-450 - Planning Proposal | p.87]. A narrow strip of land along Old Northern Road would remain zoned SP2 Infrastructure (Classified Road) [doc: PP-2024-450 - Planning Proposal | p.87] to allow for future road widening [doc: PP-2024-450 - Planning Proposal | p.87]. * **Building Height**: The maximum height limit for buildings would be reduced from 10 metres [doc: PP-2024-450 - Planning Proposal | p.9] to 9 metres [doc: PP-2024-450 - Planning Proposal | p.9]. * **Infrastructure**: The proposal also involves delivering a new local park and improvements to the local road network [doc: PP-2024-450 - Planning Proposal | p.33][doc: PP-2024-450 - Traffic and Transport Impact Assessment | p.50].

## Things to know

Residents should be aware of several findings from technical studies for the proposed development near Old Northern Road and Derriwong Road, Dural.

**Noise:** The area experiences high existing traffic noise [doc: PP-2024-450 - Acoustic Assessment | p.7]. Buildings will need specific noise control features, including improved glazing and particular floor finishes. Ventilation systems are required where windows must be closed [doc: PP-2024-450 - Acoustic Assessment | p.7]. Different bedroom floor types also impact acoustic requirements [doc: PP-2024-450 - Acoustic Assessment | p.7].

**Contamination:** Elevated hydrocarbon levels were found in surface soils at 618 Old Northern Road [doc: PP-2024-450 - Detailed Site Investigation | p.39]. Asbestos materials, including fragments and pipes, were observed at both 618 Old Northern Road and 21 Derriwong Road, with the full extent of underground pipes unknown [doc: PP-2024-450 - Detailed Site Investigation | p.39]. These impacts require management [doc: PP-2024-450 - Detailed Site Investigation | p.39]. Dam water at 618 Old Northern Road contains heavy metals and can be used for irrigation, but not discharged into waterways [doc: PP-2024-450 - Detailed Site Investigation | p.39].

**Heritage:** The proposal is near a heritage item, but it is not expected to diminish its significance or views [doc: PP-2024-450 - Heritage Impact Assessment | p.21]. No Aboriginal heritage objects or sites were found [doc: PP-2024-450 - Aboriginal Heritage Assessment | p.22].

**Other Risks:** The land is subject to bushfire risk, requiring development to meet specific guidelines [doc: PP-2024-450 - Detailed Site Investigation | p.167]. The site is not affected by flooding, tidal inundation, subsidence, or acid sulphate soils [doc: PP-2024-450 - Detailed Site Investigation | p.167].
