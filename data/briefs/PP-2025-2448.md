## What's being proposed

The Penrith City Council is proposing updates to the Penrith Local Environmental Plan 2010 (LEP 2010) [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.5]. These changes are mainly administrative, aiming to improve clarity, fix errors, delete outdated rules, and remove duplicated information within the plan [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.7][doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.5]. The goal is to ensure the LEP is current and effective, aligning with existing policies and laws [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.5].

The proposal includes changes to the written rules and maps of the LEP 2010 [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.7]. This involves updating property descriptions and making minor adjustments to land use rules and residential controls [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.8]. There are also updates to the heritage register and revisions to "Additional Permitted Use" provisions [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.8].

Some land owned by NSW Government agencies is proposed for rezoning [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.8]. For example, 90b Forestwood Drive, Glenmore Park, which is part of Mulgoa Nature Reserve, would change from a C2 zone to a C1 zone [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.37][doc: Appendix A - Proposed Penrith LEP 2010 Maps | p.12]. Parts of 207 Bennett Road, St Clair would also be rezoned from R2 to RE1 to make the R2 zoning consistent across the site [doc: Appendix A - Proposed Penrith LEP 2010 Maps | p.26]. Mapping errors are also being corrected, such as those affecting 2 John Oxley Avenue and 174 Coreen Avenue [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.81].

## What changes on the ground

Here's what changes on the ground:

*   **Zoning and Building Heights**:
    *   A portion of 207 Bennett Road, St Clair, will be rezoned from R2 to RE1 [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.10].
    *   64 Gough Street, Emu Plains, will change from SP2 to R2 zoning, with a new 8.5m building height limit [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.39].
    *   Land at the Great Western Highway, St Marys (Lot 3 DP 261871), will be rezoned from SP2 to R3, also with an 8.5m building height limit [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.39].
    *   Land at Memorial Avenue, Penrith, near the Yandhai Nepean Crossing Pedestrian Bridge (including parts of 16 and 20 Memorial Avenue), will change from SP2 to SP3 zoning and have a 12m height control [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.67].
    *   In Caddens, residential-zoned land will now have a 9m height limit, while land for water management facilities will have a 5m height limit [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.71].

*   **Lot Sizes**:
    *   A new minimum lot size of 550sqm will apply to 209a, 209b, 209c Bennett Road, St Clair, and the northern portion of 58 Smith Street, Kingswood [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.10].
    *   64 Gough Street, Emu Plains, will also have a minimum lot size of 550m2 [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.39].

*   **Land Reservation**: Properties at 2166B Castlereagh Road, Penrith, Jack Williams Drive, Penrith, and 2-16 Parkway Avenue, Glenmore Park, will be removed from the Land Reservation Acquisition map [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.10]. Land at Memorial Avenue, Penrith, will also be removed from this map [doc: Planning Proposal (March 2026) | p.67].
