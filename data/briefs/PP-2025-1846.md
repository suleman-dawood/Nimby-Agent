## Summary

This approximately 30071 m2 vacant land, owned by Weddin Shire Council, is currently classified as "Community" land and is a public reserve [doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.20]. The proposal will reclassify the land to "Operational" land and remove its public reserve status [doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.20][doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.10]. This reclassification would remove restrictions on Council's ability to manage, develop, or dispose of the land, potentially allowing for its future sale [doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.7][doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.20]. The land's R1 General Residential zoning will not change [doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.16][doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.20]. Adverse environmental impacts are considered unlikely, and economic consequences are not expected to be significant [doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.16]. There are no public infrastructure requirements [doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.16]. The land is not expected to be developed for public recreation in the future due to sufficient existing recreational facilities in Grenfell [doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.15].
