## Summary

The proposal seeks to change the land's classification from "Community" to "Operational" land under the Local Government Act 1993, and remove its public reserve status [doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.9][doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.10][doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.20]. Currently, as Community land and a public reserve, it cannot be sold. Reclassifying it to Operational land would allow Council to potentially sell the land in the future, if it resolves to do so [doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.7][doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.20]. The land was set aside as a public reserve in a 2005 subdivision but remains vacant and unused [doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.20]. Crucially, the proposal does not intend to change the land's current R1 General Residential zoning or the types of uses allowed on it [doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.16][doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.20]. The Council expects no significant economic consequences, adverse impacts on critical habitats, or public infrastructure requirements [doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.16]. It is also considered unlikely the land will be developed for public recreation due to existing facilities nearby [doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.15][doc: Weddin Shire Council Planning Proposal Reclassification of Land - Lot 133 DP 1081488 | p.16].
