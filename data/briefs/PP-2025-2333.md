# Temporary Workers’ Accommodation

**PP Number:** PP-2025-2333  
**Council:** Armidale Regional  
**Address:** []  
**Exhibition:** 2026-04-09 to 2026-05-22  
**Type:** council-led

## What's being proposed

This planning proposal, called 'Temporary Workers’ Accommodation', aims to change our local planning rules for the Armidale Regional Council area [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.7]. The main objective is to allow for and properly locate 'Temporary Workers’ Accommodation' [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.7].

If approved, the changes would:
*   Create a formal definition for 'Temporary Workers’ Accommodation' within the local planning rules [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.7].
*   Make it permissible to build this type of accommodation in certain land use zones throughout the Armidale Regional LGA [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.7]. This will be achieved by adding a new provision to Part 6 of the local planning rules [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.8].
*   Set up specific development guidelines to ensure these accommodations are located appropriately [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.7].

It's important to note that these new rules would not apply to the RU3 Forestry Zone, or any recreational and conservation zones [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.8].

## What changes on the ground

Planning Proposal No. 28 [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.51] introduces new local provisions to enable temporary workers’ accommodation in certain zonings within the Armidale Regional LGA, specifically excluding the RU3 Forestry Zone and all recreation and conservation zones [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.8]. The proposal will not rezone land from a rural zone to a residential, employment, mixed use, SP4 Enterprise, SP5 Metropolitan Centre, W4 Working Waterfront, village or tourist zone [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.55]. It will also not affect existing or proposed Employment Zones [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.55], zonings for public purposes [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.50], or conservation zones [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.53].

For instance, potential amendments to reduce minimum lot sizes for R2 Low Density Residential zones from 4,000m2 to 1,000m2, or for R5 Large Lot Residential zones from 2 hectares to 8,000m2, are explicitly noted as "Not relevant to the PP" [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.39]. Similarly, investigations into rezoning land, strengthening subdivision requirements, or increasing housing densities are also not part of this proposal [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.38][doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.36][doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.37]. The proposal focuses on a controlled approach to regulating temporary workers’ accommodation developments, rather than altering broader land use planning controls [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.10]. Future development applications (DAs) for temporary workers' accommodation will be required to demonstrate adequate public infrastructure for the specific development on the specific site [doc: Planning Proposal - Temporary Workers Accommodation - V2.1 -January 2026 - Post Gateway-Public exhibition version | p.45].
