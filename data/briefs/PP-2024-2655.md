# Additional permitted use to 131P Henley Marine Drive to permit a function centre.

**PP Number:** PP-2024-2655  
**Council:** Not specified  
**Address:** ["131P Henley Marine Drive", "Drummoyne"]  
**Exhibition:** 2026-04-13 to 2026-05-11  
**Type:** council-led

## What's being proposed

This proposal seeks to amend local planning rules to allow "function centres" as an additional permitted use at 131P Henley Marine Drive, Drummoyne [doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.7][doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.26]. Currently, the site is zoned for RE1 – Public Recreation [doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.19].

A function centre is defined as a building or place for holding events, functions, or conferences, including convention, exhibition, and reception centres [doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.20]. This change would introduce a new rule in Schedule 1 of the Canada Bay Local Environmental Plan 2013 [doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.7].

The main goals are to expand the types of employment-based uses on the site and give landowners the option to consider developing a function centre, provided a future Development Application addresses any environmental effects [doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.25]. This would help keep employment uses on the site and prevent it from being redeveloped for residential or mixed-use purposes [doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.25]. The proposal also aims to meet the increasing demand for function centres within the Canada Bay area and improve local amenities [doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.25].

It is important to note that the proposal does not change the existing objectives of the RE1 – Public Recreation zone, nor any other uses currently allowed or prohibited [doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.20]. There are also no proposed changes to building height or floor space ratio limits for the site [doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.20].

## What changes on the ground

This proposal primarily seeks to add "function centres" as a new type of use that would be allowed on the land at 131P Henley Marine Drive, Drummoyne, with council approval [doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.5][doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.26]. A "function centre" is defined as a building or place for holding events, functions, or conferences, including reception centres [doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.20]. The land currently has two zones [doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.5]. Part of the site, specifically Lot 461 in DP 752023, is zoned RE1 – Public Recreation under the Canada Bay Local Environmental Plan 2013 [doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.5][doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.19]. Lots 1, 2, and 3 in DP 1032986 are zoned Zone 2 – Environment Protection under State Environmental Planning Policy (Biodiversity and Conservation) 2021 [doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.8][doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.5]. This proposal does not change the existing zoning for either part of the site [doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.20][doc: Planning Proposal (1 April 2026) | p.7].
